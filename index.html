Controls

Play Shortcuts

  • Mouse wheel or + / - buttons to zoom
  • Drag, WASD, or arrow keys to pan
  • Space pauses and resumes
  • N starts the level intro or next level
  • Enter confirms main actions on popups
  • R retries when available
  • L opens level select
  • Click the level badge to open level select

Scoring

Fast is Better

  • No fail timer. Search as long as you need.
  • Faster clears earn more score and stars.
  • Wrong clicks reduce score but don't reveal the target.
  • Bonuses open after level 5 or 10 total stars.
