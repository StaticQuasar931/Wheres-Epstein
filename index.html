Controls

Play Shortcuts

  • Mouse wheel or buttons to zoom
  • Drag, `WASD`, or arrows to pan
  • `Space` pauses and resumes
  • `Enter` confirms the main action on popups
  • `R` retries when retry is available
  • `L` opens level select
  • Double click the level badge to open level select
  • Use the preview toggle to hide the target card

How Scoring Works

Fast is better

  • There is no fail timer. You can search as long as you need.
  • Faster clears earn more score and more stars.
  • Wrong clicks only reduce score. They do not reveal the target.
  • Bonuses open once you beat normal level 5 or reach 10 stars total.
